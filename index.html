Hello! I'm Lucie.

I'm a frontend developer living and working in Berlin. After years in media education and working as an online editor I decided to educate myself and teach myself how to code instead. In early summer 2015 I got my first job as a developer! Now I'm with the lovely people from bitcrowd.

I also spend a lot of time in Berlin's buzzing tech community, attending meetups, helping out as a coach at CSSclasses (beginner-friendly free CSS courses) and as a volunteer for CSSconf EU, which is part of the global CSS and JS conference family.

And since I obviously can't help but put things on the internet, I write and podcast for the group blog kleinerdrei.org about popculture, nerdy stuff and everyday life, and also do podcasts about series and games of the 80s and 90s - swaying between nostalgia and media criticism (all in German though).
